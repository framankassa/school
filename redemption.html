About Us

Since 2009, the School of Redemption has been a beacon of excellence in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. With over 3000 students and a dedicated team of 400 staff members, we have created a nurturing environment where students thrive academically, socially, and morally. Our motto, "If excellence is possible, good is not enough," drives everything we do.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become one of the top leading schools in Ethiopia by 2025, recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, inclusive, and inspiring learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and moral values. We empower students to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.

Our Programs

Kindergarten

Our kindergarten program focuses on play-based learning, helping young minds develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a fun and engaging environment.

Elementary School

From Grades 1 to 6, we emphasize foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking, preparing students for the challenges ahead.

High School

Our high school program offers a rigorous academic curriculum, extracurricular activities, and career guidance to help students excel in their chosen paths.
